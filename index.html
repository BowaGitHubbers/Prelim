About

Hello! BowaxArt here, a dedicated multimedia arts student and digital artist. I've always been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and technology, so I majored in multimedia arts. When I discovered the power of visual storytelling through digital mediums, my journey as a digital artist began.

As a multimedia arts student, I've honed my skills in graphic design, animation, video production, and interactive media. I enjoy experimenting with new techniques and tools in order to create visually stunning and engaging content. I enjoy pushing the boundaries of digital art to convey unique narratives, whether it's designing captivating graphics, creating immersive animations, or producing compelling videos.

Curiosity and a strong desire to create drive my creative process.
